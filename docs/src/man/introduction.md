## Bernstein Expansion

Consider a polynomial in ``n`` variables, ``x_1, \ldots, x_n`` expressed in its power form,

```math
    p(x) = \\sum_{i=0}^l a_i x^i,\qquad x = (x_1, \ldots, x_n),
```
where we use the multi-index notation. The degree of ``p`` is ``l = (l_1, \ldots, l_n)``,
